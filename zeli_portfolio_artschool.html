Portfolio-art-Ze Li

Ze Li(1990-), aka Covel Neptune, took photos, made videos and drew.

He was born in Xi'an, China, where famous film director Zhang Yimou was from. He had been struggling with maths during his school days. He enjoyed literature.

Most of his understanding of art had been self-taught.

Youku, YouTube, Vimeo/ Flickr, 500px, Behance/ WordPress/ Instagram

2008-2012

school days: video making, photography

2008 He bought his first computer by a classmate Mr Sun Lei.

2009 He was taught by Mr Sun Lei to use Photoshop, After Effects and Maya. He bought his first camera and was taught by a photography group in school.

2010 He decided to go to a training class of vfx but failed. He began to write scripts.

2011 He bought more cameras and did "street photography". He bought his second computer. He got the permission of adapting a novel into a movie. He sometimes attended classes in Beijing Film Academy.

2012 He learned how to write stories for sell in a journalism school.

2015 January to July

Title: mountains and forests of China: episode one- from Zhongnan mountain to Taohua island

Material: video

Size: maybe 30 minutes

I posted it onto Vimeo. The original file is lost.

Description: It took half a year to shoot by a JVC video camera I bought in 2013 in the UK and a phone. I worked as security staff near the mountain in my hometown from January to March in order to shoot the change of seasons in the mountain. I captured river in early spring, flowers, snow and Buddhist temples. Then from April to June I worked as an apprentice chef in Zhejiang province. I also shoot some videos about the locals. In July I finally got back to my computer and the whole video was assembled and edited in Beijing with a narrative written and read by myself.

Written on April 8, 2021

2016

Title: The wild

Material: Chinese ink on paper

Size: maybe broadsheet newspaper size

I posted the photos of it onto Behance. The page (actually two pages put together) here is a part of a 4-page cartoon. The original file is lost. You could head to my Behance for the whole story.

Description: In 2016 I somehow read a piece of news about a university student who killed his mother in Fuzhou, Fujian province. I did some research and drew a picture as a fake ad for a famous brand. I bought a bottle of ink and found it very useful to make black and white drawings. I was in Fujian from April that year. I rented from July 1st 2016 till April 1st 2017 in Fuzhou. A friend of mine from school helped to pay my rent and living expenses for nearly a whole year. I wrote a script and translated it into this 4-page, interesting cartoon. The story might not make sense. It might be inspired by the three Tin Tin cartoons, Tibet, Blue Lotus and Exploring the moon, which I purchased in the UK. In 2016 I began to put two companies’ brands a lot in my drawings.
